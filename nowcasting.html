Chapter Overview

This chapter covers Nowcasting in the context of cancer surgery prehabilitation — the use of real-time or near-real-time data to estimate a patient's current clinical status, risk profile, and trajectory. Nowcasting methods support timely clinical decision-making and enable proactive, personalised prehabilitation planning.
